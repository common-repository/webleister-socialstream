Show posts from different social media plattforms (YouTube,Instagram,Twitter,Facebook,Wordpress) on your site as site content.

== Description ==

Do you ever wanted to take all the posts from your socialmedia account and put them in a timeline on your site? 
Then this plugin does what you want. You can chose from which social plattforms you want to take your posts and show them on your Webpage. It is quite simple to include.

This plugin is written by [Webleister](https://www.webleister.ch/)

== Installation ==

1. Upload 'wl-socialstream' to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Configure the plugin in WL SocialStream -> Settings
1. Place the Shortcode [wl­socialstream] wherever you want

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How often does the SocialStream refresh the Posts? =

It will refresh every Hour as it is solved with the Wordpress Scheduler.

= Why i don't see all the Posts from the past? =

Thats because the social plattforms limits the posts in the past by number. At this moment it is not possible to get all your Posts. SocialStream will get all Posts from the time of activation.
